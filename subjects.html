Subjects we offer

English and Mathematics for NSW students in Years 7–10, aligned with NESA requirements and tailored to student goals.

English (Years 7–10)

We build strong literacy skills: reading, writing, speaking, and understanding texts. Students learn to analyse language features, structure responses, and express ideas with clarity and confidence.

  • Comprehension and close reading of varied texts
  • Paragraphing, essay writing, and extended responses
  • Persuasive, imaginative, and informative writing
  • Vocabulary, grammar, and editing skills
  • Exam strategy and time management
Enrol for English

How we teach

Small groups or one‑on‑one. Short, focused practice combined with model responses and feedback.

By year level

  • Year 7–8: foundations, sentence craft, text types
  • Year 9: analysis, comparison, building essay structure
  • Year 10: high school exam skills, stronger responses

Mathematics (Years 7–10)

Confidence with number, algebra, measurement, geometry, statistics, and probability. We emphasise problem‑solving strategies and mathematical reasoning.

  • Number skills and fractions fluency
  • Algebraic manipulation and equations
  • Measurement, ratio, and rates
  • Geometry, Pythagoras, and trigonometry (Year 9–10)
  • Statistics and probability basics
Enrol for Mathematics

How we teach

Concept‑first explanations, guided examples, then independent practice with feedback and revision.

By year level

  • Year 7–8: foundations and confidence
  • Year 9: multi‑step problems and reasoning
  • Year 10: preparation for Stage 6 pathways
